# Problem 7: Radius of Electron in Magnetic Field

**Given:**
- $V = 5000 \, \text{V}$, $B = 0.1 \, \text{T}$
- $e = 1.6 \times 10^{-19} \, \text{C}$, $m_e = 9.11 \times 10^{-31} \, \text{kg}$

---

## Solution:
1. **Find velocity ($v$):**
   $$eV = \frac{1}{2} m v^2 \implies v = \sqrt{\frac{2eV}{m}}$$
   $$v = \sqrt{\frac{2(1.6 \times 10^{-19})(5000)}{9.11 \times 10^{-31}}} \approx 4.19 \times 10^7 \, \text{m/s}$$

2. **Find radius ($R$):**
   $$R = \frac{mv}{qB} = \frac{(9.11 \times 10^{-31})(4.19 \times 10^7)}{(1.6 \times 10^{-19})(0.1)}$$
   $$R \approx 0.00239 \, \text{m} = 2.39 \, \text{mm}$$

**Answer:**
The radius is **2.39 mm**.
