# Problem 10: Magnetic Force on a Current-Carrying Wire

**Given:**
- $L = 2.0 \, \text{m}$, $I = 10 \, \text{A}$, $B = 0.5 \, \text{T}$
- Formula: $F = BIL \sin\theta$

---

## Calculations:
- **a) $\theta = 90^\circ$:**
  $F = (0.5)(10)(2.0) \sin 90^\circ = \mathbf{10 \, \text{N}}$

- **b) $\theta = 45^\circ$:**
  $F = (0.5)(10)(2.0) \sin 45^\circ = 10 \cdot \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2} \approx \mathbf{7.07 \, \text{N}}$

- **c) $\theta = 0^\circ$:**
  $F = (0.5)(10)(2.0) \sin 0^\circ = \mathbf{0 \, \text{N}}$

**Answer:**
Forces are **10 N**, **7.07 N**, and **0 N** respectively.
