# Problem 8: Lorentz Force Calculation

**Given:**
- $q = 2 \times 10^{-19} \, \text{C}$
- $B = 0.5 \, \text{T}$, $v = 10^6 \, \text{m/s}$, $\theta = 90^\circ$

---

## Solution:
The magnetic part of the Lorentz force is $F = qvB\sin\theta$:
$$F = (2 \times 10^{-19})(10^6)(0.5)(\sin 90^\circ)$$
$$F = 1 \times 10^{-13} \, \text{N}$$

**Answer:**
The magnitude of the force is **$10^{-13} \, \text{N}$**.
