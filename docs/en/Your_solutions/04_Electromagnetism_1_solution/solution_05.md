# Problem 5: Electric Field for Proton Levitation

**Given:**
- $m_p = 1.67 \times 10^{-27} \, \text{kg}$
- $q_p = 1.6 \times 10^{-19} \, \text{C}$
- $g = 9.8 \, \text{m/s}^2$

---

## Solution:
To levitate, the upward electric force ($F_e = qE$) must equal the downward gravitational force ($F_g = mg$):
$$qE = mg \implies E = \frac{mg}{q}$$
$$E = \frac{(1.67 \times 10^{-27})(9.8)}{1.6 \times 10^{-19}}$$
$$E \approx 1.02 \times 10^{-7} \, \text{V/m}$$

**Answer:**
The required electric field strength is **$1.02 \times 10^{-7} \, \text{N/C}$** (or V/m).
