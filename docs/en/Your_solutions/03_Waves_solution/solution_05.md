# Problem 5: Distance Calculation via Echo

**Given:**
- Echo time (round trip): $t = 1 \, \text{s}$
- Speed of sound in air: $v = 343 \, \text{m/s}$

---

## Solution:
The sound travels to the cliff and back to the person, meaning the total distance covered is $2d$ (where $d$ is the distance to the cliff).
$$2d = v \cdot t$$
$$d = \frac{v \cdot t}{2}$$
$$d = \frac{343 \times 1}{2} = \mathbf{171.5 \, \text{m}}$$

**Answer:** The distance to the cliff is **171.5 meters**.
