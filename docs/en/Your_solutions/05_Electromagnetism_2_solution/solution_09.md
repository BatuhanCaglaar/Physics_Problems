# Problem 9: Light Refraction Analysis

**Given:**
- Index of refraction (Air): $n_1 = 1.00$
- Index of refraction (Glass): $n_2 = 1.50$
- Angle of incidence: $\theta_1 = 30^\circ$

---

## Solution:
According to Snell's Law:
$$n_1 \sin(\theta_1) = n_2 \sin(\theta_2)$$

Substitute the values:
$$1.00 \cdot \sin(30^\circ) = 1.50 \cdot \sin(\theta_2)$$
$$1.00 \cdot 0.5 = 1.50 \cdot \sin(\theta_2)$$
$$0.5 = 1.5 \sin(\theta_2) \implies \sin(\theta_2) = \frac{0.5}{1.5} = \frac{1}{3}$$

Calculate the angle:
$$\theta_2 = \arcsin\left(\frac{1}{3}\right) \approx 19.47^\circ$$

**Answer:**
The angle of refraction is approximately **$19.47^\circ$**.
