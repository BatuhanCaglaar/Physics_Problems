# Problem 7: Analysis of Visible Light

**Given:**
- Wavelength ($\lambda$): $550 \, \text{nm} = 550 \times 10^{-9} \, \text{m}$
- Speed of light ($c$): $3 \times 10^8 \, \text{m/s}$

---

## Solution:
1. **Color Determination:**
   In the visible spectrum, $550 \, \text{nm}$ falls directly in the **Green** (specifically yellow-green) region. This is the wavelength where human photopic vision is most sensitive.

2. **Frequency Calculation ($f$):**
   Using the wave relation $c = f \cdot \lambda$:
   $$f = \frac{c}{\lambda} = \frac{3 \times 10^8}{550 \times 10^{-9}}$$
   $$f \approx 5.45 \times 10^{14} \, \text{Hz}$$

**Answer:**
The light corresponds to the color **Green** and has a frequency of approximately **$5.45 \times 10^{14} \, \text{Hz}$**.
