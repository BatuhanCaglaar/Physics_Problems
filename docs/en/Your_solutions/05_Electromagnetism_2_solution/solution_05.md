# Problem 5: Capacitor Analysis

**Given:**
- $S = 0.02 \, \text{m}^2$, $d = 5 \times 10^{-3} \, \text{m}$, $U = 500 \, \text{V}$

---

## Solution:
1. **Capacitance (C):**
   $$C = \epsilon_0 \frac{S}{d} = (8.85 \times 10^{-12}) \frac{0.02}{0.005} \approx \mathbf{35.4 \, \text{pF}}$$

2. **Energy Stored (W):**
   $$W = \frac{1}{2} C U^2 = 0.5 \cdot (35.4 \times 10^{-12}) \cdot (500)^2 \approx \mathbf{4.43 \times 10^{-6} \, \text{J}}$$

3. **Electric Field (E):**
   $$E = \frac{U}{d} = \frac{500}{0.005} = \mathbf{10^5 \, \text{V/m}}$$

4. **Force of Attraction (F):**
   $$F = \frac{1}{2} \epsilon_0 E^2 S = 0.5 \cdot (8.85 \times 10^{-12}) \cdot (10^5)^2 \cdot 0.02 \approx \mathbf{8.85 \times 10^{-4} \, \text{N}}$$
